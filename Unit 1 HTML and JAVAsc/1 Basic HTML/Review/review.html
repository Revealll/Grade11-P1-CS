Iphone 8 Review

Review of an iphone 8, a piece of technology made by apple, a company worth trillions.

Colours

The iphone 8 comes in 3 general colours

  • Silver
  • Space gray
  • Gold
I personally like the space gray colour the most, as it doesn't stand out. This iphone is a perfect size, as it fits perfectly in your hand,it still has a home button and overall it is aesthetically pleasing. It has a nice feel and is comforting.

Speed

The iphone 8 is not the slowest but also not the fastest as it is an older model at the moment. "It is a 4G device that is rated 4G LTE cat12 (603 Mbps)"

Apps

You can download whatever app you want in the appstore, but when you get your new iphone 8, it has many predownloaded apps. Some of the many include:

  • Pages
  • Numbers
  • iMovie
  • Garageband
  • Keynote
  • iTunes U
  • Clips
  • Apple Store App

Overall, the iphone 8 is a must buy and it is an amazing piece of technology that makes a persons life much easier.
